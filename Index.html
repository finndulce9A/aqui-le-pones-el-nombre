📖 Study Guide & Pronunciation

Click the speaker button to hear the correct native pronunciation of each modal verb.

Must / Mustn't

Used for strong obligations or prohibitions. Never use "to" after them, and never add "-s".

Have to / Don't have to

Used for rules and laws. The negative form means something is optional.

Should / Shouldn't

Used to give advice. Remember that the letter 'l' is silent when speaking.

Might / May

Used to talk about future possibilities or things that are not 100% certain.
